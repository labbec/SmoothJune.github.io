New Perspectives on the Analysis of Singular Stochastic PDEs

In recent years, there has been growing interest in the study of singular stochastic partial differential equations, which find applications in a wide range of fields, notably in the stochastic quantization of field theories and in the description of the critical behavior of complex systems. This 3-day workshop will focus on innovative analytical approaches to the theory of solutions for a broad class of singular SPDEs, featuring two mini-courses by renowned experts of the field and seminars from young researchers.
