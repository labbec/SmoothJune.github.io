ANR Smooth

Conference Paris 2026

This 3-day workshop is funded by the ANR project Smooth that gathers researchers in singular stochastic PDEs and dispersive PDEs with random initial data. It will consist of a mini-course by Bjoern Bringmann and six talks by other participants.

Conference Details
